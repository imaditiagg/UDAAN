UDAAN

An Initiative To Bring Together Local Tutors

READ ANALYSIS




Udaan is an initiative to bring together local tutors.

The cost of college education today is so high that many young people are giving up their dream of going to college, while many others are graduating deeply in debt. Our AIM is to bring together local tutors, students, NGO support and government services under one window. We call out for all the enthusiastic volunteers to come out and impart their knowledge to the young generation of
our country.

"Education is directly proportional to Equality"





WHY ARE WE DOING THIS ?

We are motivated individuals. India gave us the opportunity to study and flourish. All we are doing is giving it back the opportunities it gave us.
Professional

For us, commitment matters. We work extra hard to give everyone the power of professionalism.

           
Respect New Ideas

We are an idea ourselves. So we respect new budding ideas. You are most welcome to come and give new ideas.

           
Support

We understand that we will be dealing with people from different areas with different education level. So we do our best to provide every type of support.
