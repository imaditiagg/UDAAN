ABOUT US

Udaan is an initiative where we give young motivated volunteers a chance to serve as part-time teachers in rural areas. The motive of udaan is to reduce the problem of educational inequity in india, and provide education to all.

Local Tutors

Bring together motivated volunteers which can tutor the children around. 		            

NGO Support

We connect different NGO's which facilitate with the logistics and connection between students and local tutors. 		            

Government Support

Local tutors have a verified id-card, which they can use to avail special perks at various government centers. Government also funds this entire process through NGO's.
